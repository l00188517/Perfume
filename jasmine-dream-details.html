Jasmine Dream

An intoxicating and sensual fragrance designed for unforgettable nights and moonlit encounters.

An Intoxicating Scent for Sensual Nights

Jasmine Dream is a tribute to the mysterious allure of the night. It captures the essence of a hidden garden where jasmine flowers release their most potent and seductive aroma only after dusk. This fragrance is crafted for moments of intimacy and high glamour.

It is a bold, confident scent that feels both timeless and modern. The perfume is designed to merge with the wearer's skin, creating a uniquely personal and powerfully attractive aura. It is the perfect final touch for an evening ensemble, promising to leave a memorable trail.

This is the ultimate evening fragrance. It's so rich and long-lasting, but not overwhelming. It makes me feel incredibly confident and sensual. I wore it to a gala and felt like the belle of the ball!

Chloe Dubois

Eveningwear Enthusiast
